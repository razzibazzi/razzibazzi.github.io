About Us

Yasha is a milk tea cafe named after the Bible verse Yeshua, founded in 2019 by a licensed electrical engineer and her brother during the pandemic. Despite being a small business, Yasha attracts many customers and has expanded to two branches. Initially, the cafe only offered milk tea but later added Muslim foods like pater to their menu.
